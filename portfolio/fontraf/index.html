OUR PRODUCTS

THERMOPLASTIC EQUIPMENTS

Thermoplastic road marking is one of the most common types of road marks. Hot applied, thermoplastic markings excel through improved durability, excellent reflection properties.
Moreover it can be used for delineation with an acoustic and rumbling effect.

See more

AIRLESS EQUIPMENTS

The airless system is a type of road marking that combines high profit with excellent qualit y. Due to the low dispersion rates of application, when repainting the roads, there’s no need to mill the previous markings.
Cold painting is much easier and safer than hot painting.

See more

PRE-HEATERS

Fontraf’s pre-heaters ensure a fast fusion of the thermoplastic material while keeping the heavy duty feature, characteristic of Fontraf’s machinery.
The pre-heaters are built in refractory steel, insulated in ceramic blanket, encased in brushed aluminum.

ABOUT US

Touguidraulica, Componentes e Acessórios, Lda. was created in 2011 and is dedicated to the construction of road marking machines.
Our brand, Fontraf Road Marking Machines is internationally known for the quality and reliability of its products. Fontraf Road Marking Machines has a vast portfolio of standard equipments. These can be adapted and tailored to our client’s needs.
All projects are made in total coordination with the client. This ensures a customized end- result, with excellent qualities of durability, performance and reliability. We also provide technical assistance to our valued clients.
